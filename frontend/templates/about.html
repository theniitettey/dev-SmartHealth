About Smart Health Sync

Clinical Decision Support
for Modern Healthcare

Smart Health Sync is an AI-powered clinical decision-support platform developed as a final-year research project at the University of Ghana. It helps verified physicians analyse standard blood-panel biomarkers and receive transparent, explainable diagnostic insights for six chronic conditions — supporting faster, more informed clinical workflows in resource-constrained settings.

Our Purpose

Healthcare Benefits

Augmented Diagnosis
Supports physicians by flagging patterns across 24 standard biomarkers, reducing manual interpretation time while keeping the clinician in control.
Explainable AI
Every prediction includes a human-readable explanation of why the condition was suggested — building trust and supporting clinical review.
Resource-Conscious Design
Built for hospitals and clinics where specialist access is limited, enabling faster triage using routine laboratory data.

How It Works

Clinical Workflow

1. Doctor Registration
Physicians register with professional credentials. Accounts remain pending until verified by the Super Admin.
2. Enter Biomarkers
Verified doctors input 24 standardised blood-panel values from laboratory screenings into the diagnosis engine.
3. Review & Report
The system returns a predicted condition with confidence score, clinical explanation, and downloadable reports for the patient record.
Vision & Impact

Future of Clinical AI in Ghana

Smart Health Sync demonstrates how responsible AI can augment — not replace — clinical judgement. By combining verified physician access, transparent explanations, and structured diagnostic history, the platform lays groundwork for safer AI adoption in African healthcare systems. Planned enhancements include EHR integration, audit logging, and AI-generated clinical recommendations.

Verification First

Only credentialed, Super Admin–verified physicians can run diagnoses — ensuring professional oversight at every step.

Transparency by Design

Every diagnosis explains why the prediction was made — linking biomarker patterns to the suggested condition in plain clinical language.

Academic Research Prototype — Not for Clinical Use
This system was developed solely for final-year academic evaluation at the University of Ghana. It is not an FDA-cleared or CE-marked medical device. All predictions must be verified by a licensed medical practitioner. Do not use this system for unsupervised clinical decision-making.
